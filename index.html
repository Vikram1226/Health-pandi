About Us

A wellness journey, led by people who lived it.

Lucky's Wellness is a Herbalife Independent Associate practice founded by certified coaches Dr. R. Ramesh Pandi (B.E., DFN., D.ACU.) and R. Abirami Sundari (M.A., B.Ed., DFN.) — a husband-and-wife team based in Madurai. Coach Ramesh transformed his own health at age 49, losing 23 kg, and together with Abirami has guided hundreds of families to do the same.

Our Mission

Help every family lose or gain weight through good nutrition — sustainably, and without crash diets.

Our Vision

A healthier India — one balanced plate, one informed family, one transformation at a time.

Our Promise

We care, we educate, we coach. One-to-one, every step — from your first analysis to your goal.
